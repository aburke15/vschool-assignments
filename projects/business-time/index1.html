About:


I'd like to think of myself as a tech enthusiast. I feel like I feel that I know enough about most of it anyway. This is just a site that I have to document my thoughts on a few smartphones other than that it's nothing too fancy. It's a pretty basic page if you ask me. I remember getting my first Android smartphone back in 2011 it was called the Samsung Infuse 4G. That's when my interest in tech really took off. I also remember watching several YouTube reviews on that old Samsung phone and I'd get really pumped about hearing what specs the phone had and what not. I've found that if you're really into tech then specs do matter. With that said I would have to say based off of my own speculation that most tech enthusiast (when talking about smartphones) lean towards Android. The Android ecosystem is very diverse, so diverse that they typically require powerful hardware to keep them speedy and efficient. That is what makes Android such a cool platform, because for tech geeks it's pure power. That's a lot of what keeps me interested in tech. I like to read about those powerful specs. They always say "specs aren't everything," well I whole-heartedly disagree.
